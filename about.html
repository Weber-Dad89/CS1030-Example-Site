About Cynsity

I create immersive electronic music that connects with emotions and vibes.

My music is inspired by a variety of electronic dance music genres.

My Top 5 EDM Genres

  • 1. Future Bass
  • 2. Trip-Hop
  • 3. House
  • 4. Drum & Bass
  • 5. Chillstep

From rap battles in high school and the military to the rave scene, Cynsity found a passion for creating beats that invoke emotion and transcend words.

Specializing in future bass and trip-hop, Cynsity produces music that makes you feel—whether it’s joy, excitement, nostalgia, or pure movement.

Listen to *Snowfall*, *Ride The Wave*, and explore *The Origin*, Cynsity’s debut EP.
